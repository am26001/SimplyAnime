Genres: What Is Your Favorite Anime?

Just like there are different types of genres for books and television shows, this also applies to anime. These include but are not limited to the following anime genres: sci-fi, romance, shonen, psychological, mystery, horror, supernatural, magic, fantasy, slice of life, drama, comedy, adventure, and action. There are also subgenres such as cyberpunk, game, demons, martial arts, historical, isekai, military, mecha, music, parody, police, post-apocalyptic, school, sports, super power, tragedy, vampire, and more! The good thing is that if you do not like one genre, you can watch another genre that appeals to you. If you think a romance anime is too slow, then an anime that involves action might be the best for you. Personally, my favorite genres are action and horror. So, do not be afraid to explore the variety of anime genres. Although there are MANY anime genres, I will narrow the list down for simplicity. You will learn about the folllowing anime genres: action, adventure, horror, psychological, romance, and movies! Please feel free to see each anime synopsis and any other relevant information that might interest you in watching it.
