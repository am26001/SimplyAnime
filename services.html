Services

Where can you watch anime? There are a lot of places where you can watch anime shows and movies. These include but are not limited to: VRV, Crunchyroll, Funimation, Netflix, and Hulu. All these services are the main ones where people end up watching anime. My favorite streaming services are VRV and Crunchyroll. These two services contain a lot of content which makes it worth paying for a subscription. For instance, VRV does not only have anime shows but cartoons as well. On the other hand, Crunchyroll allows you to read manga, comics or graphic novels originating from Japan. Below you will find a breakdown of the prices and benefits for each of the common streaming services. Enjoy this information so you can watch your favorite anime and movies.

VRV

VRV offers one membership. To enroll in a membership, one must use the 30 day free trail. Watch it all! Get newest episodes first, exclusive series, and ad-free access to everything on VRV. A $25.95 value for only $9.99/month after your free trial ends. Although you can have a membership, VRV has over 20,000 hours of free content, which updates regularly. Some channels have exclusive content or will provide first access to content for members who go Premium. With VRV Premium, you will have access to the following channels: Cartoon Hangover, Crunchyroll, HIDIVE, MONDO, Rooster Teeth, and VRV Select. Upcoming seasons and series like VRV Select's HarmonQuest, Cartoon Hangover’s Bravest Warriors and Mondo’s Lastman will be exclusive to their respective channels. These channels will only be available on VRV and the exclusive ones are Cartoon Hangover, MONDO, and VRV Select.

Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll offers three memberships. The first membership is "fan". The cost is $7.99/month. Benefits include: no ads, unlimited access to the Crunchyroll library, new episodes one hour after Japan, access to digital manga (manga is only available in English), and stream on 1 device at a time. The second membership is "mega fan". The cost is $9.99/month. Benefits include: no ads, unlimited access to the Crunchyroll library, new episodes one hour after Japan, access to digital manga, stream on 4 devices at a time, offline viewing, first access to special Crunchyroll Expo events and lotteries, and $15 off $100 purchase in the Crunchyroll Store every 3 months. The third membership is "ultimate fan". The cost is $14.99/month. Benefits include: those mentioned in the second membership, $25 off $100 purchase in the Crunchyroll Store every 3 months, annual swag bag, and access to an exclusive limited-run Hime Nendoroid figure.

Funimation

Funimation offers three memberships. The first membership is "premium". The cost is $5.99/month. Benefits include: ad-free access to entire Funimation library when and where available, subs and dubs, and 2 simultaneous streams. The second membership is "premium plus". The cost is $7.99/month. Benefits include: ad-free access to entire Funimation library when and where available, subs and dubs, 5 simultaneous streams, download for offline viewing on supported mobile devices, exclusive members-only events, early access to special Funimation shop sales, free shipping on Funimation shop orders over $20, and members-only offers. The third membership is "premium plus ultra". The cost is $99.99/year. Benefits include: those mentioned in the second membership, and annual anniversary gifts from Funimation. This service does offer a lot!

Netflix

Netflix offers three memberships. The first membership is "basic". The cost is $8.99/month. Benefits include: 1 number of screens you watch at the same time, 1 number of phones or tablets you can have downloads on, ulimited movies and TV shows, and watch on your laptop, TV, phone and tablet. The second membership is "standard". The cost is $13.99/month. Benefits include: 2 number of screens you watch at the same time, 2 number of phones or tablets you can have downloads on, ulimited movies and TV shows, watch on your laptop, TV, phone and tablet, and HD available. The third membership is "premium". The cost is $17.99/month. Benefits include: 4 number of screens you watch at the same time, 4 number of phones or tablets you can have downloads on, ulimited movies and TV shows, watch on your laptop, TV, phone and tablet, HD available, and ultra HD available. Netflix contains not just anime but regular TV shows and movies.

Hulu

Hulu offers three memberships. The first membership is "hulu". The cost is $5.99/month. Benefits include: streaming library with thousands of TV episodes and movies, most new episodes the day after they air, access to award-winning Hulu originals, watch on your TV, laptop, phone, or tablet, up to 6 user profiles, and watch on 2 different screens at the same time. The second membership is "hulu (no ads)". The cost is $11.99/month. Benefits include: streaming library with thousands of TV episodes and movies, most new episodes the day after they air, access to award-winning Hulu originals, watch on your TV, laptop, phone, or tablet, up to 6 user profiles, watch on 2 different screens at the same time, no ads in streaming library, and download and watch. The third membership is "hulu + live tv". The cost is $64.99/month. Benefits include: those mentioned in the second membership, live tv with 65+ top channels, live tv guide to navigate channels, and record live tv with 50 hours of cloud dvr storage. Hulu contains not just anime but regular TV shows and movies.
